Eatogether 

        Eatogether takes real person’s phone number to register. It targets to help 
        a small circle of friends, usually less than 50,to share dining availability 
        and preference. You can tell certain portion or all your friends when you 
        are available for a meal and what do you want to eat. 
        Before, 30 mins before dinner, you call 4 friends asking whether they want 
        to eat dinner together. A and B are going. Then you are the busy middle man 
        to coordinate what time works for everyone and what restaurant to go. At end, 
        because A and B have different preference for food, you don’t know who you 
        should eat with.
        Now, 6 hours before dinner, you and your friend A and B changes status for 
        dinner on Eatogether. 3 hours before dinner, you find both A and B are free 
        at 6 tonight and would like to go to Chinese restaurant, the same as you. 
        Since you know everything now, you can just call or text them, saying 
        “Hey, 6pm today, China First. Let’s go.”

Carpooler

Carpooler opens an space that shares carpool events. You can create a carpool event no matter you are a driver or passenger. Or you can respond to other events as a driver or passenger. Carpooler helps you manage your created event and your requested event. You can confirm your event and cancel your event easily. It makes your wish to find a perfect carpool easier and more efficient. You also get to know people using Carpooler and make friends with people who create or share events. So go ahead and become part of it.

Journey

Journey is an app that keeps track of places you have been. You can save information like description and photos of the place, it could be a good restaurant or a nice park you feel happy to stay. If you want to go that place again, the direction will be shown and if you want to refresh your happy memory, you can review what you have saved in that place. You can also wander around and the map will keep track of your route, make you aware of how long you have traveled and where you have been.

Get Up

Have trouble getting up from your bed? Alarm clock seems not to help at all? You might need another choice. Get Up app will make you get up without struggle. Music, light, color puzzle and inspirational words will make you want to get up on time naturally. You don't need multiple alarms any more, one is enough for you just like your old fashioned bedside alarm. Go get up with it and it will become part of your life.

Face detection GUI

Face detection GUI written in Java swing. It simply performs real time face detection functionality based on video and can also do detection on single image. You can choose between different types of Haar cascades classifiers and use them for different purposes. You can also save images with detected faces easily.

Robotic arm simulator GUI

RM501 robot arm simulator GUI simulates robotic arm movements and display them in GUI. You can input the target joint angles of robotic arm and it will go to the destination position you input. You can also store several positions prehand and it will go to these positions in sequence. You can change the speed of arm movement and make the arm do special task like drawing a circle or fetching an object. GUI is written in Matlab using GUIDE.

Shipping container inspection

There are lots of shipping containers passing through the custom channel every single day. Custom officers have to check every single of them when they pass by, which is very tiring and time consuming. Using software to automatically inspect container and extract useful information can be very efficient. The method to inspect container back area and components on it is based on image and vision. Areas of interest are extracted in order and the final result combines all extracted informations.
